# Business Memo

## Key Findings

1. Low web activity is associated with higher churn.
2. Customers with multiple support complaints appear more likely to churn.
3. Lower customer ratings correlate with churn risk.
4. Loyalty program participation may reduce churn.
5. Campaign response should be analyzed before increasing discount spending.

## Recommendations

### Priority 1

Target customers showing declining website activity.

### Priority 2

Resolve support issues faster.

### Priority 3

Improve loyalty enrollment.

### Priority 4

Review discount strategy to avoid training customers to wait for offers.

### Priority 5

Create proactive retention campaigns for customers with poor ratings.
